Quick Answer

What is a State Farm Guest Payment Key Code?

A State Farm Guest Payment Key Code is a unique 10-digit number printed on your paper bill that lets you pay your insurance online without logging into an account. For Auto and Payment Plan bills, find it at the bottom of page 1 in the tear-off section. For Homeowner, Fire, Life, and Health bills, it's at the top-right corner. You'll also need your phone number on file and date of birth to complete the payment. If you can't locate your key code, call 1(888) 933-1113 for immediate help.

Step-by-Step

How to Pay Your State Farm Bill Without Logging In

Follow these 4 steps to complete your guest payment in under 5 minutes.

What You'll Need Before Starting

  • Your 10-digit Guest Payment Key Code from your paper bill
  • The phone number associated with your State Farm account
  • Your date of birth for identity verification
  • A payment method — bank account (ACH) or debit/credit card

  1. Locate Your Guest Payment Key Code

    Find the 10-digit key code on your paper bill. Auto and Payment Plan bills: bottom tear-off of page 1. Homeowner, Fire, Life, or Health bills: top-right corner.

    Tip: Can't find it? Call 1(888) 933-1113 — support can retrieve it for you.

  2. Go to the State Farm Guest Payment Page

    Navigate to the official State Farm guest payment portal on their website. Look for the "Pay Without Logging In" or "Guest Payment" option on the bill pay section.

    Tip: Prefer guided help? A specialist at 1(888) 933-1113 can walk you through the portal in real time.

  3. Enter Your Verification Details

    Input the 10-digit key code, your phone number on file with State Farm, and your date of birth. These three items verify your identity without requiring a login.

  4. Review Amount & Submit Payment

    Confirm the amount due, select your payment method (bank account or card), and submit. Save or screenshot your confirmation number — it's your proof of payment.

    All transactions are secured with bank-level encryption.
